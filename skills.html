Things I've leared while Dirt Biking

Although dirt biking is meant to be a hobby, I have learned crucial and valuble skills doing so. Perseverance is one of these skills. I have fallen over and over and broken a bone or two while dirt biking. Every time that I have fallen down, I have learned to get back up and keep going no matter how hard it may be. I have been able to apply perseverance in many other aspects of my life and it is something that has truly set me apart from others.

Another Valuble Skill... Leadership

For 6 months, I led a group of 20 people in an organization in Colombia. I was in charge of setting goals with the group and motivating everyone to work hard in order to acheive those goals. After the first 3 months, our results had increased by 200% and continued to rise thoughout the last 3 months.
